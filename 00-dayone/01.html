First Day Notes:

NOTES

  • When using emmet, class is notated as a "." and ID is by "#".
  • HTML stands for Hypertext Markup Language.
  • DOCTYPE states the type of our documents. In this case, HTML.
  • Our HTML tag denotes our language to be English, per the lang="eng".
  • The head tag houses metadata, or detailed data about our doc.
  • UTF-8 specifies character encoding. Each character (letter, number, or even spaces) have a numeric value.
  • Viewpoint notes the device we are reading our document on.
  • Title reflects the name that displays on our browser tab.
  • Link allows us to connect to other files, such as our stylesheet (CSS).
  • A style tag can help us inject css into our document. *Not the best practice.
  • The body tag houses all the other tags that we want to display onto our browser. This will be what the user sees!
